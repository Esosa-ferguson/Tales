About Us

NutriPlanner is your modern companion for healthy living. We believe food is more than fuel—it's a story of tradition, health, and care. Our platform empowers you to plan meals that fit your lifestyle and nutrition goals, with expert guidance and a seamless experience.

Why NutriPlanner?

  • Personalized meal planning for every goal
  • Evidence-based nutrition advice
  • Easy-to-use tools for tracking and feedback
  • Support from a team of wellness experts

Tales: A Journey Through Meals,
Every dish tells a story. Let’s write yours.

At Tales, we believe that food is more than just fuel — it’s a powerful storyteller. Each meal speaks of tradition, health, intention, and care. That’s why we built Tales: a digital companion to help you plan and enjoy meals that align with your lifestyle and goals.

Whether you’re focused on wellness, managing nutrition, or simply staying organized, Tales helps you create a meaningful path — one plate at a time.
